Feature Engineering

After compiling the dataset for your study, you will often need to do an additional step of feature engineering. This process involves creating new features or modifying existing ones to better understand our data.

  • Often taken before modeling
  • Done while preparing the data to feed it to the model in the right format
  • Creates new information from existing information in your dataset

Feature engineering ka matlab hai ki aapke data mein jo information already hai, usse naye features banane ka process. Ye modeling se pehle kiya jata hai taki data ko better tarike se samajh sake aur model ko behtar format mein feed kar sake.

Hidden Information in Raw Data

  • The raw data gives you information evident to you
  • There is usually more information hidden in the raw data
  • Feature Engineering is like being a detective, searching for clues hidden within your data
  • Often, the most crucial insights are not apparent in the raw data

Raw data mein bahut saari information chupi hoti hai jo direct dikhti nahi hai. Feature engineering ek detective ki tarah kaam karta hai, jahan aap data mein chupe huye clues ko dhoondte hain. Kai baar, sabse important insights raw data mein seedhe dikhte nahi hain.

Examples of Feature Engineering

Example 1: Date and Time Features

Dates and times contain rich information that can be extracted as new features:

From the Purchase Date, we can extract:

  • Day of Week: Sunday, Saturday, Monday, etc.
  • Is Weekend: True/False or 1/0 flag indicating weekend vs weekday
  • Month: January, February, etc.
  • Quarter: Q1, Q2, Q3, Q4
  • Is Holiday: Could be checked against a holiday calendar

Importance of Domain Expertise

  • Feature engineering isn't just about technique
  • It requires understanding your field and domain
  • Domain expertise is needed to engineer useful features
  • You need to know what to look for based on field knowledge
  • Features should be constructed to test hypotheses or generate insights

Feature engineering sirf technique nahi hai, ismein domain knowledge bahut zaroori hai. Aapko apne field ke baare mein acchi jaankari honi chahiye taki aap sahi features bana sakein jo meaningful insights de. Jaise retail mein weekend sales ka pattern important ho sakta hai, usi tarah alag-alag fields mein alag-alag features important hote hain.

Recap

  • Feature engineering is creating new features from existing data
  • It helps uncover hidden insights not apparent in raw data
  • Common techniques include:
    • Date/time transformations
    • Mathematical calculations (like BMI)
    • Aggregations (like transaction totals)
  • Domain expertise is crucial for effective feature engineering
  • Feature engineering is typically done before modeling

Feature engineering ka recap yeh hai ki hum existing data se naye features banate hain jo hidden insights reveal karte hain. Isme date/time transformations, mathematical calculations, aur aggregations jaise techniques use hoti hain. Domain expertise bahut zaroori hai effective feature engineering ke liye. Modeling se pehle ye step kiya jata hai.
